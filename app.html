Welfare Evaluation App

The interactive app allows you to compute welfare comparisons between the Sustainable Convergence and Productivist Convergence scenarios under your own parameter assumptions. Adjust the social discount rate, utility specification, climate damage parameters, and leisure curvature to explore how the results change.

App hosted on Streamlit Community Cloud. If the embedded view is slow to load, use the full-screen link above. Data files can be downloaded from the Data page.

What the App Computes

Utility Specifications

Additive (baseline): Separable log consumption plus CRRA leisure. The leisure weight χ is calibrated from the 2025 baseline work–leisure split, independently of consumption levels.

CES: Consumption and leisure enter a constant elasticity of substitution composite. The elasticity parameter ε controls the degree of complementarity or substitutability.

Ecological: A CES composite of damage-adjusted consumption and environmental quality, combined with a leisure term. This specification gives direct utility weight to the environment.

Key Parameters

  • Social discount rate (r) — default 2%; range 0.5%–5%
  • Output damage (φ) — default 12% per °C
  • Amenity damage (γ) — default 13.33% per °C
  • Leisure curvature (σ) — default 1 (log leisure); range 0–3
  • CES elasticity (ε) — default 0.8 (mild complements)
