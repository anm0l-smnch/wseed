All datasets, figures, and replication materials are freely available for download. The welfare evaluation app uses the same underlying data and can be accessed on the App page.

Scenario Data

Annual region-level projections (2025–2100) of per-capita consumption, per-capita labour hours, and cumulative temperature change for eight world regions under two scenarios: Sustainable Convergence (SC) and Productivist Convergence (PC).

Data Structure

The scenario Excel files contain two sheets: SC (Sustainable Convergence) and PC (Productivist Convergence). Each sheet covers 76 annual time steps (2025–2100) across eight regions, with the following variables:

  • c — per-capita consumption (USD, 2020 PPP)
  • h — per-capita annual labour hours (hrs/yr)
  • ΔTinc — year-on-year temperature increment (°C)

Eight regions: Europe (EURO), North America & Oceania (NAOC), Latin America (LATAM), MENA, Sub-Saharan Africa (SSAF), Russia & Central Asia (RUCA), East Asia (EASA), South & South-East Asia (SSEA).
